Hello :D

For some time now, I have been interested in physics, mathematics and biology. My research so far focuses on studying the mechanisms that give rise to different developmental processes in living systems, particularly blood and the evolution of cells. I usually use tools associated with nonlinear dynamical systems and numerical programming and simulation.

However, recently I have become much more interested in technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing and electronics. Therefore, I found myself transitioning to Scientific Machine Learning, implementing APIs in the cloud, automatizing tasks, and developing prototypes and trying out sensors.


If you want to know more about me, go to the About Me section.
